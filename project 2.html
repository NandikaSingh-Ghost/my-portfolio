<html> is the root tag of an HTML document.

Bold Tag: The tag is used to make text bold. It is often used to emphasize important information or to create headings.

Italic Tag: The tag is used to make text italic. It is often used to indicate emphasis or to denote titles of works such as books, movies, or songs.

Underline Tag: The tag is used to underline text. It is often used to indicate hyperlinks or to emphasize important information.

Strike Tag: The tag is used to create a strikethrough effect on text. It is often used to indicate deleted or outdated information.
